Privacy Policy

Dusty: Clean Sweep by Starr Apps

Overview

Dusty: Clean Sweep ("the App") is a casual puzzle game developed by Q- Starr / Starr Apps. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

The App does not:

  • Require an account or login
  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Display advertisements
  • Share data with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores game progress (scores, coins, unlocked skins) locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Game Center

The App integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Game Center data is handled.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
